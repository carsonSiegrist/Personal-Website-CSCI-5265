Postpals

Overview

Postpals is a multiplayer side-scroller game created during a Game Jam. The objective is to work together to deliver a letter safely to its destination.
Created in Godot, the game allows for local multiplayer (shared keyboard) and the demo is complete with movement, reactive doors, levers, buttons and an end condition. Playable in browser.
